Frequently Asked Questions

What is a deepfake?

+

A deepfake is a synthetic media created using artificial intelligence, typically to replace someone's likeness in an existing video or audio.

How can I detect a deepfake?

+

Deepfakes can be detected using specialized algorithms that analyze inconsistencies in facial expressions, blinking patterns, and video/audio artifacts.

Is this website free to use?

+

Yes, this platform offers free tools for uploading videos and detecting deepfakes. Some advanced features may require a subscription.

Why is deepfake detection important?

+

Deepfake detection is crucial to combat misinformation, prevent misuse of identity, and protect against privacy violations.
